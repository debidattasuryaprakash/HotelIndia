Plan to plant 2 million trees

Revenues that we earn should not be the prior importance of our hotel but they should be playing a role in the development of nature and its beauty. During the building of the hotels, the area had to be cleared from scrubs and proper infrastructure but this resulted in deforestation scaling to a small label. So to help our mother earth with each order whether ordered or canceled, we will be planting 2 trees.
